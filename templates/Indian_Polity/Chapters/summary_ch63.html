Chapter 63 — National Commission for Women (NCW)

Foundation
  • NCW Act 1990; Jan 31, 1992 operational
  • Statutory; HQ New Delhi; MoWCD
  • First Chair: Jayanti Patnaik
  • India ratified CEDAW 1993
Composition
  • 1 Chair + 5 Members = 6 (ALL women)
  • Central Govt appoints (not President)
  • 3-year tenure; re-appointment allowed
  • No SC inquiry for removal
Functions
  • Review safeguards; recommend legislation
  • Investigate complaints; suo motu
  • Civil Court powers; inspect institutions
  • Advisory only (not binding)
Constitutional Articles
  • Art 15(1): no sex discrimination; 15(3): special provisions
  • Art 39(d): equal pay; Art 42: maternity
  • Art 51A(e): FD renounce derogatory practices
  • 106th Amendment 2023: 33% Parliament reservation
Key Laws
  • Dowry Prohibition Act 1961
  • PWDVA 2005; POCSO 2012
  • POSH 2013 (Vishaka 1997)
  • Maternity Benefit Amendment 2017 (26 wks)
