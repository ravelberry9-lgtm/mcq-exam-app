🗺️ Ch78: Special Provisions for Some States — Summary

  • Art.370 — J&K special status — abrogated August 5, 2019; J&K (UT with legislature) + Ladakh (UT without legislature)
  • Art.371 — Maharashtra/Gujarat: development boards for backward regions
  • Art.371A — Nagaland: customary law protected from Parliament; Art.371G — Mizoram: same
  • Art.371B — Assam: tribal areas committee; Art.371C — Manipur: hill areas committee
  • Art.371D/E — Andhra Pradesh/Telangana: equitable regional opportunities, APAT
  • Art.371F — Sikkim: merger provisions (36th Amendment 1975)
  • Art.371H — Arunachal Pradesh: Governor special responsibility for law/order
  • Art.371I — Goa: minimum 30 MLAs; Art.371J — Karnataka: Hyderabad-Karnataka region (98th Amendment 2013)
