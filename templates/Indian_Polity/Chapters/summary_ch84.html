Coalition Government (Ch84)
Coalition governments arise when no single party has majority. India's multi-party system, regional diversity, and caste fragmentation drive coalition formation. First coalition: Janata Party (Morarji Desai, 1977). Era of unstable coalitions: 1989–1999 (5 PMs in 10 years). NDA II (Vajpayee, 1999–2004) was the first coalition to complete a full term. UPA I&II (Manmohan Singh, 2004–2014). BJP won single-party majority in 2014 (282) and 2019 (303). In 2024, BJP won 240 seats — below majority; NDA coalition dependency returned (TDP + JD-U crucial). INDIA alliance formed in 2023 won 234 seats in 2024.
