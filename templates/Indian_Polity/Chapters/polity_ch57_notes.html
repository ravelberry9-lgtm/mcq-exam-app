Chapter 57 — Central Information Commission (CIC) & RTI Act 2005

కేంద్ర సమాచార కమిషన్ | Right to Information Act 2005

0. Historical Background & RTI Act 2005

నేపథ్యం

Freedom of Information Act 2002 (weak, not operative) → RTI Act 2005 replaced it.

RTI Act 2005: June 15, 2005 passed; October 12, 2005 fully operative.

Right to Information: Implied in Article 19(1)(a) — Freedom of Speech (SC held).

CIC (Central) + SIC (State) — two-tier information commission structure.

1. RTI Act Key Provisions & Exemptions

ముఖ్య నిబంధనలు

Section 8: Exempt — national security, cabinet papers, personal info, commercial confidence, foreign relations, fiduciary, parliamentary privilege, etc.

30 days: Normal response; 48 hours: life/liberty cases; 40 days: third party involved.

Section 4: Pro-active/suo motu disclosure — Public Authorities voluntarily disclose info.

RTI Amendment 2019: CIC/SIC tenure + salary — Central/State Govt prescribes.

2. CIC Composition & Appointment

సంఘటన

Chief Information Commissioner (CIC) + Information Commissioners (ICs).

President నియమిస్తారు — PM, Leader of Opposition (or largest opposition leader), PM-nominated Cabinet Minister committee recommendation.

ICs maximum number Parliament Act defines (flexible).

Eminent public life, wide knowledge/experience in govt, law, science, technology, social service, management, journalism, mass media.

3. Tenure, Removal & Service Conditions

పదవీకాలం

RTI Act original: 5 years; max age 65 (no re-appointment).

RTI Amendment 2019: Central Govt tenure/salary prescribes — flexibility added.

President removes — SC inquiry తర్వాత (misbehaviour/incapacity).

Salaries: Consolidated Fund of India.

4. Powers & Functions of CIC

ముఖ్య విధులు

Second appeal from information applicants/third parties.

Suo motu: Section 4 compliance examine చేయగలదు.

Penalty: Rs.250/day default (max Rs.25,000); disciplinary action recommend.

Civil Court powers: summons, documents, affidavits.

Decisions binding on Public Authorities.

5. Public Information Officers (PIOs) & First Appeal

PIOs

PIO (Public Information Officer): Every Public Authority — designated officer.

APIO (Assistant PIO): Sub-district, sub-divisional levels.

First Appellate Authority: Senior officer in same Public Authority (within 30 days).

Second Appeal: CIC (Central) or SIC (State).

6. State Information Commission (SIC) & Implementation

SIC

SIC: State Chief Information Commissioner + State Information Commissioners.

Governor appoints — CM + Leader of Opposition + CM-nominated Cabinet Minister committee.

SIC handles second appeals from State Public Authorities.

RTI Amendment 2019: State Govt tenure/salary prescribes for SIC.

7. Miscellaneous / Key Facts

Key Facts

RTI Act: 2005

Operative: October 12, 2005

Response time: 30 days (normal); 48 hours (life/liberty)

Penalty: Rs.250/day; max Rs.25,000

Appointment: President (CIC); Governor (SIC)

RTI Amendment: 2019
