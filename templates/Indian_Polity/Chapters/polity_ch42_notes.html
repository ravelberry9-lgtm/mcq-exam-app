Chapter 42 — కేంద్రపాలిత ప్రాంతాలు & ఢిల్లీ / Union Territories & Delhi

Art.239–242 | Art.239AA (Delhi) | Part VIII | 8 UTs | J&K Reorganisation 2019

Section 1 — Union Territories: Introduction
Part VIII (Art.239-242): Union Territories — directly administered by Central Government.
Current 8 UTs: Delhi (NCT), Puducherry, Chandigarh, Dadra & Nagar Haveli and Daman & Diu, Lakshadweep, Andaman & Nicobar Islands, Jammu & Kashmir, Ladakh.
UTs with Legislature: Delhi + Puducherry + J&K.
UTs without Legislature: Chandigarh, Dadra-Daman, Lakshadweep, Andaman & Nicobar, Ladakh.
Section 2 — Art.239-240: Administration
Art.239(1): Every UT administered by President through an Administrator (appointed by President).
LG titles: Lieutenant Governor = Delhi, Puducherry, A&N, J&K, Ladakh; Administrator = Chandigarh (Punjab Governor), Lakshadweep, Dadra-Daman (Goa Governor).
Art.239A: Parliament may create legislature/Council of Ministers for certain UTs — basis for Puducherry legislature.
Art.240: President may make regulations for UTs without legislature (= force of Act of Parliament).
Art.239B: Administrator of UT (with legislature) may promulgate Ordinances when legislature not in session.
Art.239AB: President's Rule in UT with legislature if constitutional machinery fails (analogous to Art.356).
Section 3 — Art.239AA: Delhi NCT Special Status
Art.239AA: Inserted by 69th Constitutional Amendment 1991 (effective Feb 1, 1992). Delhi = National Capital Territory (NCT).
Delhi Assembly: Can legislate on State List + Concurrent List subjects EXCEPT: (1) Public Order; (2) Police; (3) Land.
LG powers: Must act on CoM advice; can refer to President only for Union matters/security issues.
106th Amendment 2023: Union Parliament given power over 'services' in Delhi (overruled SC 2023 judgment).
Section 4 — Delhi LG vs CM Disputes
SC 2018 (5-judge): LG has no independent decision-making power; must act on CoM advice on non-reserved matters.
SC 2023 (Const. Bench): Delhi govt has power over 'services' — LG must act on CoM advice on services.
GNCTD Amendment 2023: Parliament overruled SC; restored Union control over services in Delhi.
Section 5 — J&K 2019 & Ladakh
J&K Reorganisation Act 2019: Bifurcated J&K State → (1) J&K UT (with Legislature); (2) Ladakh UT (without Legislature).
Art.370 abrogated: Presidential Order August 5, 2019. Art.35A simultaneously removed.
SC 2023: Upheld Art.370 abrogation — Art.370 was temporary provision.
Ladakh: UT without legislature; LG administers directly. Largest UT by area.
Section 6 — Key Provisions
Art.241: Parliament may constitute HC for a UT. Delhi HC = Parliament law.
HC jurisdictions: A&N = Calcutta HC; Lakshadweep = Kerala HC; Chandigarh = Punjab & Haryana HC.
Chandigarh: Capital of both Punjab and Haryana; UT administered by Punjab Governor.
Dadra & Nagar Haveli + Daman & Diu: Merged into single UT in January 2020 (Dadra and Nagar Haveli and Daman and Diu Merger of Union Territories Act 2019).
