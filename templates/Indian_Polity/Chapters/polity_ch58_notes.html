Chapter 58 — Central Vigilance Commission (CVC)

కేంద్ర అప్రమత్తత కమిషన్ | CVC Act 2003

0. Historical Background & CVC Act 2003

నేపథ్యం

K. Santhanam Committee 1964 — CVC ఏర్పాటు recommend చేసింది.

Executive order 1964: CVC first established (non-statutory).

Vineet Narain case 1997 (SC): CVC statutory status mandate.

CVC Act 2003: Statutory status; operative September 2004.

1. Constitutional/Statutory Status & Framework

Statutory Body

CVC: Statutory body — CVC Act 2003 (Parliament legislation).

Constitutional body కాదు — Constitution mention లేదు.

Multi-member body: Central Vigilance Commissioner (CVC) + 2 Vigilance Commissioners.

Independence: President removes after SC inquiry — strong independence.

2. CVC Composition & Appointment

సంఘటన

Central Vigilance Commissioner (Chair) + 2 Vigilance Commissioners = 3 total.

President నియమిస్తారు.

Appointment committee: PM + Home Minister + Leader of Opposition (Lok Sabha).

3. Tenure, Removal & Service Conditions

పదవీకాలం

4 సంవత్సరాలు లేదా age 65 వరకు — whichever earlier.

President removes — SC inquiry తర్వాత (misbehaviour/incapacity).

No re-appointment; salaries Consolidated Fund of India.

4. Powers & Functions of CVC

ముఖ్య విధులు

Central Govt employees corruption/misconduct complaints inquire.

CBI inquiries supervise (corruption cases).

Prosecution sanction recommendations (Group A officers).

Recommendations advisory — Govt bind కాదు.

Annual Report President కి → Parliament.

5. CVC and CBI Supervision

CBI Supervision

CVC, CBI corruption cases supervise చేస్తుంది — single directive.

CBI FIR (corruption) before filing: CVC permission/advice required (Group A).

Vineet Narain 1997: CBI single directive — CVC supervision mandatory for SPE (Special Police Establishment) cases.

CVC, Delhi Special Police Establishment Act 1946 amendments through.

6. Vigilance Commissioners & Chief Vigilance Officers

CVOs

CVO (Chief Vigilance Officer): Each Ministry/Department/PSU — designated senior officer.

CVO: Day-to-day vigilance; reports to CVC.

CVC, CVOs supervise + training + guidelines ఇస్తుంది.

CVC Instructions: All Ministries, PSUs, Banks — follow చేయాలి.
