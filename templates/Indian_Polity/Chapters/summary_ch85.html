Anti-Defection Law (Ch85)
Added by 52nd Amendment 1985 as Tenth Schedule. Triggered by "aaya ram gaya ram" defection culture (Gaya Lal, Haryana 1967). Grounds: voluntary quit of party membership, OR voting/abstaining against party whip. Exception: merger with 2/3rds of legislature party agreeing. Split (1/3rd) exemption deleted by 91st Amendment 2003. Independent member joins any party = disqualified. Nominated member must join within 6 months. Speaker/Chairman decides — subject to judicial review (Kihoto Hollohan 1992 — also struck down Para 7 barring judicial review). Nabam Rebia 2016: Speaker facing no-confidence notice cannot decide. 91st Amendment 2003 also capped CoM at 15%.
