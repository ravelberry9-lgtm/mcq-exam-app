🗳️ Ch80: Elections — Summary

  • Art.326 — Universal Adult Suffrage; voting age 18+ (61st Amendment 1988, originally 21)
  • Art.325 — Single electoral roll; no separation by religion/race/caste
  • Art.324 — ECI: superintendence of elections; Art.329: bar to court interference
  • RPA 1951 — primary elections law; Sec.123 corrupt practices; election petitions within 45 days
  • EVM — first limited use 1982; nationwide 2004; manufactured by BEL + ECIL
  • VVPAT — first used 2013 (Nagaland); paper slip shows vote for 7 seconds
  • Candidate limits — LS: 25+ years; RS: 30+; expenditure limit Rs.95 lakh (large states LS)
  • By-election — within 6 months of vacancy (unless <1 year remains)
