Chapter 90 – Landmark Judgements: Quick Summary

Basic Structure

  • Kesavananda Bharati 1973 (7:6) — basic structure cannot be amended
  • Minerva Mills 1980 — struck down unlimited amending power of 42nd Amendment
  • Waman Rao 1981 — 9th Schedule laws post-1973 are reviewable

Art.21 Expansion

  • Maneka Gandhi 1978 — procedure must be just, fair, reasonable; golden triangle
  • Olga Tellis 1985 — right to livelihood
  • Puttaswamy 2017 — privacy is FRs (9-judge, unanimous); ADM Jabalpur overruled
  • Common Cause 2018 — right to die with dignity; advance directives

Reservations

  • Champakam Dorairajan 1951 → 1st Amendment Art.15(4)
  • Indra Sawhney 1992 — 50% ceiling, creamy layer, no reservation in promotions
  • Nagaraj 2006 — SC/ST promotion reservation with quantifiable data
  • Janhit Abhiyan 2022 — EWS 10% reservation upheld (3:2)

Decriminalisation

  • Navtej Johar 2018 — S.377 read down; same-sex relations decriminalised
  • Joseph Shine 2018 — S.497 adultery struck down
  • Shayara Bano 2017 — triple talaq unconstitutional

Recent Cases

  • NJAC 2015 — collegium restored (4:1)
  • Shreya Singhal 2015 — S.66A IT Act struck down
  • Sabarimala 2018 — women's temple entry upheld (4:1; referred to 9-judge bench)
  • Anuradha Bhasin 2020 — internet access = FRs
  • Electoral Bonds 2024 — scheme struck down unanimously
