Chapter 64 – National Commission for Minorities (NCM)

NCM Act 1992 • Statutory Body • 6 Notified Minorities

Foundation
NCM Basics
National Commission for Minorities Act 1992 — statutory (NOT constitutional); HQ New Delhi
Operational: 1993; First Chair: Justice Mohammad Sardar Ali Khan
Replaced non-statutory Minorities Commission (set up 1978 under MHA)
Under Ministry of Minority Affairs (created 2006; earlier under MHA)
Minorities notified by Central Govt; currently 6: Muslims, Christians, Sikhs, Buddhists, Zoroastrians, Jains (2014)
Composition & Appointment
Structure
1 Chairperson + 1 Vice Chairperson + 5 Members = 7 total
All 7 must be from notified minority communities
Central Government appoints (NOT President)
3-year tenure; re-appointment allowed; Central Govt removes (no SC inquiry)
Salaries from Central Govt grants (not Consolidated Fund directly)
NCM Chair: NOT a deemed NHRC member (unlike NCW, NCSC, NCST, NCBC)
Functions & Powers
Key Functions
Review constitutional/legal safeguards; recommend remedial measures
Monitor Central + State minority welfare schemes
Civil Court powers for inquiries; suo motu action
Annual Report → Central Govt → Parliament
Recommendations advisory only (not binding)
Monitors Waqf property management; NCMEI (educational institutions separate body)
Constitutional Provisions
Articles
Art 25-28: Freedom of Religion; Art 29: Right to conserve language/script/culture
Art 30(1): Minorities right to establish + administer educational institutions
Art 30(2): State shall not discriminate in giving aid to minority institutions
Art 30(1A) (44th Amend 1978): Compensation for acquired minority institutions
Art 350A (DPSP): Mother tongue instruction at primary level for linguistic minorities
Key Judgments
Cases
TMA Pai Foundation 2002 (11-judge): Minority = State-wise; right to establish + administer institutions
P.A. Inamdar 2005: Unaided institutions not bound by State reservation → 93rd Amendment Art 15(5)
Bal Patil 2005: Jains = minority; SC can't compel notification; notified 2014
Azeez Basha 1967: AMU not minority institution (Central Act origin)
Quick Revision
NCM Act 1992; operational 1993 1+1+5 = 7 (all minorities) Central Govt appoints 3 years; re-appointment allowed 6 notified: Muslim+Christian+Sikh+Buddhist+Parsi+Jain Jains added 2014 Art 30 = minority educational institutions NCM Chair: NOT NHRC deemed member TMA Pai 2002 = minority determination State-wise Ministry of Minority Affairs (2006) Sachar Report 2006 = Muslim socio-economic Advisory (not binding)
