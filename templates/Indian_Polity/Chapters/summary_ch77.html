🏛️ Ch77: Special Provisions for Certain Classes — Summary

  • Part XVI — Art.330–342A: Special provisions for SC, ST, OBC, Anglo-Indians
  • Art.330/332 — SC/ST reservation in Lok Sabha (84 SC + 47 ST) and State Assemblies
  • Art.341/342 — President specifies SCs/STs; Parliament can include/exclude by law
  • Art.340 — President may appoint BC Commission (Kalelkar 1953, Mandal 1979)
  • Indra Sawhney 1992 — 27% OBC upheld; 50% ceiling; creamy layer for OBCs; no reservation in promotion
  • Art.331/333 (abolished) — Anglo-Indian seats abolished by 104th Amendment 2020
  • Art.338/338A/338B — NCSC, NCST, NCBC — constitutional bodies for SC, ST, OBC
  • 105th Amendment 2021 — Art.342A — States can identify own OBC lists
