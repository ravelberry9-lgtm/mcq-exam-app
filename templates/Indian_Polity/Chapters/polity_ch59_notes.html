Chapter 59 — Lokpal and Lokayuktas

లోక్‌పాల్ మరియు లోకాయుక్తలు | Lokpal Act 2013

0. Historical Background & Lokpal Movement

నేపథ్యం

Ombudsman concept: Sweden origin; India L.M. Singhvi coined 'Lokpal' (1963 First ARC).

First Administrative Reforms Commission (Morarji Desai, 1966): Lokpal recommend చేసింది.

Parliament bills: 1968, 1971, 1977, 1985, 1989, 1996, 1998, 2001, 2005 — none passed.

Anna Hazare movement 2011 → Lokpal Act 2013 (passed December 18, 2013).

1. Lokpal Act 2013 & Constitutional Framework

Statutory Framework

Lokpal and Lokayuktas Act 2013: Statutory body (Not constitutional).

Operative: January 16, 2014.

Lokpal: Central level; Lokayukta: State level (States mandate — 365 days).

UN Convention Against Corruption (UNCAC) 2003 — India ratified 2011 → Lokpal mandate.

2. Lokpal Composition & Appointment

సంఘటన

Chairperson: 1 + Members: up to 8 = max 9 total.

50% judicial members (HC/SC Judges); 50% non-judicial.

50% SC/ST/OBC/minorities/women members.

President appoints — PM, LoP (Lok Sabha), Speaker (Lok Sabha), CJI (or SC Judge nominee), eminent jurist committee.

3. Tenure, Removal & Service Conditions

పదవీకాలం

5 సంవత్సరాలు లేదా age 70 వరకు — whichever earlier.

President removes — SC inquiry తర్వాత.

No re-appointment; salaries Consolidated Fund of India.

4. Powers & Functions of Lokpal

ముఖ్య విధులు

PM, Ministers, MPs, Group A/B/C/D employees corruption/misconduct complaints inquire.

Investigation: Preliminary — 30 days; Full — 180 days (extend to 6 months).

Prosecution: Special Court referral; attachment/confiscation of property.

CBI direction: Refer investigation; CBI Director concurrent superintendence.

Interim relief: Attachment/provisional attachment during inquiry.

5. Lokpal Jurisdiction

Jurisdiction

PM: Included — but specific safeguards (full bench, 2/3 majority).

Ministers, MPs: Included (conduct in Parliament excluded — Article 105).

Group A/B/C/D Central employees: Included.

Judiciary (HC/SC Judges): Excluded from Lokpal jurisdiction.

State Govts: Excluded — Lokayukta covers states.

6. Lokayuktas — State Level

Lokayukta

Lokpal Act: States set up Lokayuktas within 365 days.

Maharashtra first Lokayukta: 1971 (V.S. Malimath).

Lokayuktas: State CM, Ministers, MLAs, Group A employees — jurisdiction varies.

No uniform Lokayukta structure — States own laws.
