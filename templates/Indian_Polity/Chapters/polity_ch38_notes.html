Chapter 38 — మునిసిపాలిటీలు / Municipalities

74th Amendment 1992 | Art.243P–243ZG | Part IXA | 12th Schedule | Urban Local Bodies

Section 1 — 74th Amendment: History
74th Constitutional Amendment Act 1992: June 1, 1993 నుండి effective. Urban Local Bodies (ULBs) కి constitutional status. Part IXA + 12th Schedule add.
Companion to 73rd Amendment: 73rd = Rural (Panchayats); 74th = Urban (Municipalities). Same year passed (1992).
Background: Narasimha Rao government reform. India urbanization increased — ULBs need constitutional recognition. Earlier = only State discretion.
Effective date: June 1, 1993 = Urban Local Government Day. National Urban Development Day.
Section 2 — Art.243P-Q: Types of Municipalities
Art.243Q: Three types of Municipalities: (1) Nagar Panchayat (transitional area — rural to urban transition); (2) Municipal Council (smaller urban area); (3) Municipal Corporation (larger urban area).
'Transitional area': Area transitioning from rural to urban. State law specifies. Nagar Panchayat = least urbanized.
Other names: Municipal Corporation = Mahanagar Palika; Municipal Council = Nagar Parishad; Nagar Panchayat = Notified Area Committee (some states).
Art.243P(e): 'Municipality' = institution of self-government constituted under Art.243Q.
Section 3 — Art.243R-S: Composition & Ward Committees
Art.243R: Municipality composition = State law. Direct elections for ward members. Representation = Lok Sabha/State Legislature MPs (elected + nominated) + Subject matter experts (without vote) + Chairpersons of Committees.
Art.243S: Ward Committees = population 3 lakh + municipalities mandatory. Smaller municipalities = State law. Ward Committee = one ward or group of wards.
Chairperson election: State law decides method (direct or indirect).
Section 4 — Art.243T: Reservation
SC/ST reservation: Proportional to their population (same as Panchayats — Art.243D).
Women reservation: Not less than 1/3 of total seats (Art.243T(3)). Many States = 50%.
Chairpersons: Reservation = State law (SC/ST + Women).
OBC: Not mandatory in Constitution — State discretion.
Rotation: Reserved seats = rotate different wards each election.
Section 5 — Art.243U-V: Duration & Disqualifications
Art.243U: Municipality duration = 5 years from first meeting. Dissolution → elections within 6 months. New body = remaining period only.
Art.243V: Disqualifications = State law. Minimum age = 21 years (same as Panchayats).
Art.243U(4): If remaining period < 6 months = no election mandatory.
Section 6 — Art.243W-X: Powers, Finance, SFC
Art.243W: State law = Municipality powers + functions. 12th Schedule = 18 subjects (not automatically devolved).
Art.243X: Financial powers = State law (taxes, duties, fees, grants).
State Finance Commission (Art.243I): Reviews Panchayats + Municipalities — same SFC covers both.
12th Schedule (Art.243W): 18 subjects including Urban planning, Regulation of land use, Construction, Public health, Parks/gardens, Public amenities, Street lighting, Slum improvement, Fire services.
Section 7 — Art.243Y-ZG
Art.243Y: State Finance Commission = Municipalities financial position also review.
Art.243Z: Municipality accounts audit = State law (State Auditor General).
Art.243ZA: State Election Commission (SEC) = Municipal elections superintendence (same SEC as Panchayats — Art.243K).
Art.243ZB: Application to UTs = President notification.
Art.243ZC: NOT applicable to: Scheduled areas, Tribal areas (same as Panchayats exception).
Art.243ZD: District Planning Committee (DPC) = every district (integrate Panchayat + Municipal plans). 4/5 members = elected (proportional Panchayat + Municipal population).
Art.243ZE: Metropolitan Planning Committee (MPC) = Metropolitan areas (population 10 lakh+). 2/3 members = elected. State law constitute.
Art.243ZG: Bar on courts in Municipal elections (like Art.329 Parliament, Art.243O Panchayats).
Section 8 — 12th Schedule & Key Facts
12th Schedule: 18 subjects for Municipalities (Art.243W). Urban planning, Regulation of land use, Construction buildings, Roads/bridges, Water supply, Public health, Slum improvement, Urban forestry, Cultural/educational/aesthetic aspects, Burials/cremations, Cattle pounds, Street lighting, Parking, Bus stops, Public amenities.
73rd vs 74th Amendment: 73rd = Rural (Panchayats, Part IX, 11th Schedule-29 subjects); 74th = Urban (Municipalities, Part IXA, 12th Schedule-18 subjects).
Ward Committees: Art.243S — mandatory for municipalities with population 3 lakh+. Sub-municipality level governance.
District Planning Committee: Art.243ZD — integrate rural+urban planning at district level. Chairperson = MLA or district head (State law).
