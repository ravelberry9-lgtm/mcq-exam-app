Chapter 38 — మునిసిపాలిటీలు / Municipalities

74th Amendment 1992 | Art.243P–243ZG | Part IXA | 12th Schedule | Urban Local Bodies

Section 1 — 74th Amendment: History
74th Constitutional Amendment Act 1992: June 1, 1993 నుండి effective. Urban Local Bodies (ULBs) కి constitutional status. Part IXA + 12th Schedule add.
Companion to 73rd Amendment: 73rd = Rural (Panchayats); 74th = Urban (Municipalities). Same year passed (1992).
Background: Narasimha Rao government reform. India urbanization increased — ULBs need constitutional recognition. Earlier = only State discretion.
Effective date: June 1, 1993 = Urban Local Government Day. National Urban Development Day.
Section 2 — Art.243P-Q: Types of Municipalities
Art.243Q: Three types: (1) Nagar Panchayat (transitional area); (2) Municipal Council (smaller urban area); (3) Municipal Corporation (larger urban area).
Art.243P(e): Municipality = institution of self-government constituted under Art.243Q.
Section 3 — Art.243R-S: Composition & Ward Committees
Art.243R: Direct elections for ward members. Representation = MPs, experts (no vote), Committee chairpersons.
Art.243S: Ward Committees mandatory for population 3 lakh+.
Section 4 — Art.243T: Reservation
SC/ST: Proportional to population.
Women: Not less than 1/3 of total seats (Art.243T(3)).
OBC: Not mandatory — State discretion.
Section 5 — Art.243U-V: Duration & Disqualifications
Art.243U: 5 years from first meeting. Dissolution → elections within 6 months.
Art.243V: Disqualifications = State law. Minimum age = 21 years.
Section 6 — Art.243W-X: Powers, Finance
Art.243W: 12th Schedule = 18 subjects (not automatically devolved).
Art.243X: Financial powers = State law (taxes, duties, fees, grants).
Section 7 — Art.243Y-ZG
Art.243ZA: State Election Commission = Municipal elections superintendence.
Art.243ZD: District Planning Committee — integrates Panchayat + Municipal plans. 4/5 members elected.
Art.243ZE: Metropolitan Planning Committee = Metro areas (10 lakh+). 2/3 elected.
Art.243ZG: Bar on courts in Municipal elections.
74th Amendment = 1992 Effective June 1, 1993 Part IXA = Art.243P-243ZG 12th Schedule = 18 subjects 3 types of Municipalities Women = 1/3 reservation 5-year tenure Ward Committee = 3 lakh+ MPC = 10 lakh metro DPC = Art.243ZD
