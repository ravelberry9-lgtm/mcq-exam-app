Chapter 53 — National Commission for Backward Classes (NCBC)

జాతీయ వెనుకబడిన తరగతుల కమిషన్ | Article 338B (102nd Amendment 2018)

0. Historical Background & Evolution

ఏర్పాటు నేపథ్యం

Kaka Kalelkar Commission 1953 — 1st OBC commission; report rejected by Govt.

Mandal Commission 1979 (B.P. Mandal) — 27% OBC reservation recommended; implemented 1990 (VP Singh Govt).

Indra Sawhney vs Union of India (1992): 27% OBC valid; Mandal upheld; 50% cap total reservation.

Indra Sawhney: Permanent OBC Commission ఏర్పాటు mandate.

NCBC Act 1993 — statutory NCBC ఏర్పాటు.

102nd Amendment 2018 — NCBC రాజ్యాంగ హోదా; Article 338B.

1. NCBC Act 1993 (Pre-Constitutional)

NCBC 1993

Indra Sawhney judgment mandate follow అవడానికి NCBC Act 1993.

Statutory body మాత్రమే — Non-Constitutional.

NCBC 1993: OBC Central list review/update చేయడానికి.

102nd Amendment 2018 తర్వాత: NCBC Act 1993 రద్దు — Article 338B రాజ్యాంగ NCBC తీసుకొచ్చింది.

2. 102nd Amendment 2018 & Article 338B

Constitutional NCBC

102nd Constitutional Amendment Act 2018: Article 338B జోడించారు.

NCBC Constitutional body అయింది — NCSC (Art.338), NCST (Art.338A) వలె.

Article 338B: NCBC power — OBC welfare safeguards investigate, monitor, advise, report.

Article 342A (102nd Amendment): Central OBC list — President specifies; Parliament amendment.

3. NCBC Composition & Appointment

సంఘటన

Chairperson: 1

Vice-Chairperson: 1

Members: 3

మొత్తం: 5 (Chairperson + VP + 3 Members)

President నియమిస్తారు — Parliament act ప్రకారం.

4. Powers & Functions of NCBC

ముఖ్య విధులు

OBC communities raaJ్యాంగ safeguards investigate, monitor, evaluate.

OBC socio-educational advancement recommend చేయడం.

Civil Court powers — summon witnesses, documents.

Annual Report President కి → Parliament/State Legislatures.

Central OBC List update చేయాలంటే: NCBC recommend → Parliament amend చేయాలి.

5. OBC Reservation Framework

Reservation Framework

Mandal Commission 1979: 27% OBC reservation recommended.

Indra Sawhney 1992: 27% upheld; 50% cap total reservation.

OBC reservation: Central Govt services 27%.

Creamy layer: OBC (non-SC/ST) family income > Rs.8 lakh per year — excluded from reservation.

Creamy layer: SC/ST కి apply కాదు — OBC కి మాత్రమే.

6. NCBC and Central/State Lists

Central OBC List

Article 342A (102nd Amendment): Central OBC list — President specifies through Parliament.

State OBC lists: State Govts maintain — state-specific reservations.

NCBC, Central list inclusions/exclusions review చేస్తుంది.

105th Amendment 2021: States + UTs కి own OBC lists maintain చేసే హక్కు restore చేసింది.
