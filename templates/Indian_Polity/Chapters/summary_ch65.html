Chapter 65 — National Commission for Safai Karamcharis (NCSK)

Foundation
  • NCSK Act 1993; statutory; Ministry of Social Justice
  • 1+1+3=5 (all SC); Central Govt appoints; 3 years
  • Sunset clause — Parliament must renew
  • NOT NHRC deemed member
Manual Scavenging
  • Manual handling of excreta from dry latrines/rails/sewers
  • SC communities (Valmiki/Bhangi); violates Art 17+21+23
  • SKA 2014 (SC): Rs.10L sewer death compensation
  • 58K+ identified (widely seen as undercount)
PEMSR 2013
  • Replaced 1993 Act; broad definition
  • 1yr/50K (first); 2yr/1L (subsequent); employer liable
  • Rehabilitation: Rs.40K+training+loan+scholarship+housing
NCSK Functions
  • Monitors PEMSR; investigates sewer deaths
  • Monitors SRMS rehabilitation scheme
  • Civil Court powers; advisory only
