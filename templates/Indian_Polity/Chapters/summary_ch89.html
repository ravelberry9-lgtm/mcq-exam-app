Chapter 89 – NCRWC: Quick Summary

Constitution

  • Set up: Feb 22, 2000 (executive order, NDA/Vajpayee)
  • Chair: Justice M.N. Venkatachaliah (ex-CJI); 11 members total
  • Report: March 31, 2002 — 249 recommendations
  • Congress + Left boycotted; 24 sittings + 13 regional consultations

Key Recommendations

  • Constructive vote of no-confidence (German model)
  • CoM cap at 10% (implemented as 15% by 91st Amendment 2003)
  • Anti-defection decisions to ECI not Speaker (pending)
  • Whip only for confidence/money bills (pending)
  • National Judicial Commission (tried as NJAC — struck down 2015)
  • All India Judicial Service (pending)
  • Art.356 safeguards; simultaneous elections; RTI

Implemented

  • 86th Amendment (Art.21A — RTE) 2002
  • 91st Amendment (CoM 15%; deleted split provision) 2003
  • NOTA — SC order 2013
  • Asset disclosure by candidates — SC/ECI 2003
