National Integration (Ch87)
National integration = unifying India's diverse peoples into one nation. Challenges: communalism (religious division), regionalism (states reorganisation 1956, linguistic nationalism), casteism (Art.17 untouchability abolition), terrorism/Naxalism/insurgency. National Integration Council (NIC): PM-chaired, est. 1961. Constitutional provisions: Preamble (unity/integrity), Art.1 (Union), Art.14/15, Art.17, Art.25-28, Art.51A(e) (harmony duty — 42nd Amendment 1976). Measures: NIC, NFCH (1992), Ekta Diwas (Oct 31, Patel), All India Services, Kendriya Vidyalayas. Sardar Patel integrated 562 princely states.
