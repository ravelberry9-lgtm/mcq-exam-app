0. Historical Background & Evolution

నేపథ్యం

ST constitutional protections: Articles 15, 16, 46, 342 (ST list), Fifth Schedule, Sixth Schedule.

1950-2003: SC+ST combined — Article 338 Commissioner → 65th Amendment 1990 combined commission.

89th Amendment 2003: NCSC (Art.338) + NCST (Art.338A) — separate constitutional bodies.

NCST: ST-specific issues — tribal rights, forest rights, displacement, PESA, FRA.
