Electoral Reforms (Ch82)
Key reform bodies: Tarkunde Committee 1974 (non-official), Goswami Committee 1990, Indrajit Gupta Committee 1998 (state funding). 61st Amendment 1988 lowered voting age to 18. T.N. Seshan (CEC 1990–96) enforced MCC strictly and introduced EPIC (1993). SC landmark rulings: ADR 2002 (candidate disclosures), Lily Thomas 2013 (immediate disqualification), PUCL 2013 (NOTA), Electoral Bonds struck down February 2024. EVMs nationwide from 2004; VVPAT nationwide from 2019. CEC appointment law 2023 removed CJI from selection committee. One Nation One Election proposed but not implemented.
