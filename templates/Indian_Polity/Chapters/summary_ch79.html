🏛️ Ch79: Political Parties — Summary

  • National Party criteria — 6% in 4+ states + 4 LS seats OR 2% LS seats (11) from 3+ states OR State party in 4 states
  • State Party criteria — 6% + 2 seats OR 3% seats OR 1 LS/25 LS seats OR 8% votes
  • Sec.29A RPA 1951 — registration of political parties with ECI
  • Election Symbols Order 1968 — governs symbol allotment and disputes
  • India: multi-party system — over 2,500 registered; 6-8 national parties; coalition governments common since 1989
  • Electoral Bonds — struck down by SC: February 15, 2024 (ADR case)
  • Constitution — does not explicitly mention political parties (only 10th Schedule anti-defection)
