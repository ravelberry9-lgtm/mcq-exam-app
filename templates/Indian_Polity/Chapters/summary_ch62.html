Chapter 62 — National Human Rights Commission (NHRC)

Foundation
  • PHRA 1993; operative Oct 12, 1993
  • Statutory (not constitutional); HQ New Delhi
  • Paris Principles 1991; GANHRI 'A' status
  • First Chair: Justice Ranganath Misra
Composition (2019)
  • Chair (retired CJI) + 5 Members (incl. 1 woman)
  • Deemed: NCSC+NCST+NCW+NCBC chairs
  • President appoints; 6-member committee
  • Only retired judges (not sitting)
Tenure & Removal
  • 3 years or age 70; no re-appointment
  • President removes after SC inquiry
  • Salaries: Consolidated Fund of India
  • Post-retirement bar on Govt employment
Jurisdiction
  • State actor HR violations; suo motu; 1-year limit
  • No Armed Forces jurisdiction
  • Advisory (not binding); can approach SC/HC
  • Can visit jails, detention centres etc.
SHRC
  • Chair = retired HC Chief Justice
  • Governor appoints; CM+Speaker+HM+LoP
  • Parallel to NHRC (not subordinate)
  • Same tenure: 3 yrs or age 70
