Foreign Policy (Ch88)
India's foreign policy determinants: geography, history, economy, security, ideology. Art.51 DPSP: promote international peace. Panchsheel (Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence) signed with China 1954. NAM: Bandung 1955; formally founded Belgrade 1961 (Nehru, Nasser, Tito, Sukarno, Nkrumah). Indo-Soviet Treaty 1971; Pokhran I 1974; Pokhran II 1998 (NFU declared). SAARC founded 1985. Indo-US nuclear deal 2008. Post-1991: Look/Act East Policy, QUAD, SCO (India joined 2017), G20 Presidency 2023, ISA (India-France, 2015). India is largest UN peacekeeping troop contributor; seeking UNSC permanent seat (G4).
