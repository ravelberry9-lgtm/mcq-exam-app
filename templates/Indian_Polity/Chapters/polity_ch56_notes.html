Chapter 56 — National Commission for Protection of Child Rights (NCPCR)

జాతీయ శిశు హక్కుల రక్షణ కమిషన్ | CPCR Act 2005

0. Historical Background & CPCR Act 2005

నేపథ్యం

UN Convention on Rights of the Child (UNCRC) 1989 — India ratified 1992.

CPCR (Commissions for Protection of Child Rights) Act 2005 — Parliament enacted.

NCPCR operative: March 2007.

Child = person below 18 years of age (CPCR Act definition).

1. Child Rights in Indian Constitution

రాజ్యాంగ నిబంధనలు

Article 21A (86th Amendment 2002): Free + Compulsory Education (6-14 years) — Fundamental Right.

Article 24: Child Labour prohibition (hazardous employment below 14 years).

Article 39(e): No child abuse by economic necessity.

Article 39(f): Children develop healthy manner — DPSP.

Article 45 (as amended): Early Childhood Care and Education (below 6 years).

2. NCPCR Composition & Appointment

సంఘటన

Chairperson: 1 + Members: 6 = మొత్తం 7.

కనీసం 2 మహిళా సభ్యులు mandatory.

Central Govt నియమిస్తుంది (President కాదు — NCW వలె).

Members: child health, education, welfare, development, juvenile justice, disabled children experts.

3. Tenure, Removal & Service Conditions

పదవీకాలం

3 సంవత్సరాలు.

Chairperson: max age 65; Members: max age 60.

Central Govt తొలగింపు (SC inquiry కాదు).

Salaries: Consolidated Fund of India.

4. Powers & Functions of NCPCR

ముఖ్య విధులు

Laws, policies, programmes child rights perspective examine; recommendations.

Child rights violation complaints examine; suo motu cognizance.

Children's Court గా function చేయగలదు (serious child rights violations).

Civil Court powers: witnesses summon, documents, oath evidence.

Annual Report Central Govt కి → Parliament.

5. Right to Education (RTE Act 2009) & NCPCR

RTE Act 2009

RTE Act 2009: Article 21A implement — 6-14 years free + compulsory education.

NCPCR: RTE Act implementation monitor చేయగలదు; complaints examine.

25% seats EWS/disadvantaged children private unaided schools.

No-detention policy (till 8th standard) — RTE Amendment 2019 lo modified.

6. State Commissions (SCPCR) & Implementation

SCPCR

CPCR Act 2005: State Govts SCPCR (State Commission for PCR) ఏర్పాటు చేయాలి.

SCPCR: State-level child rights protection; NCPCR model follow.

SCPCR Chairperson + Members: State Govt appoints.

NCPCR + SCPCR: complementary — national + state level.
