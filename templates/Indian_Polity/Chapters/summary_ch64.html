Chapter 64 — National Commission for Minorities (NCM)

Foundation
  • NCM Act 1992; operational 1993
  • Statutory; HQ New Delhi; MoMА (2006)
  • 1+1+5=7; all from minority communities
  • 6 notified: Muslim+Christian+Sikh+Buddhist+Parsi+Jain
Appointment & Tenure
  • Central Govt appoints; 3 years; re-appointment OK
  • No SC inquiry for removal
  • NCM Chair NOT NHRC deemed member
  • Advisory (not binding)
Constitutional Articles
  • Art 29: conserve language/script/culture
  • Art 30: establish + administer educational institutions
  • Art 25-28: Freedom of Religion
  • Art 350A DPSP: mother tongue instruction
Key Cases
  • TMA Pai 2002: minority = State-wise
  • Inamdar 2005 → 93rd Amendment Art 15(5)
  • Bal Patil 2005: Jains minority; notified 2014
  • Azeez Basha 1967: AMU not minority institution
