1. IOC, Olympics & International Bodies

International Olympic Committee (IOC)
HQ: Lausanne, Switzerland
Founded: 1894 by Pierre de Coubertin (to revive ancient Greek Olympics)
Mission: "Building a Better World through Sport"
New IOC President (2025): Kirsty Coventry (Zimbabwe) — elected in Greece; former Olympic champion swimmer; first woman ever to lead the IOC

Paris Olympics 2024

Host: Paris, France | Date: July 26 – August 11, 2024
India's Contingent: 117 athletes
India's Medal Tally: 6 medals — 1 Silver + 5 Bronze

3. FIFA World Cup 2026

2026 FIFA World Cup
Host nations: Canada, Mexico, USA (tri-nation hosting; first time 3 countries co-host)
Format: First expanded WC with 48 teams (from 32 previously)
Asian qualification: At least 8 AFC (Asian Football Confederation) teams can qualify

First non-host nation to qualify: Japan
— Won 2-0 over Bahrain; Daichi Kamada + Takefusa Kubo scored
— Japan's 8th consecutive World Cup appearance (since 1998)

5. Key Retirements & Personal Milestones

Vandana Katariya — Hockey Retirement

Sport: Women's Hockey
International matches: 320 (most-capped in Indian women's hockey)
Goals: 158
Senior debut: 2009
Historic: First & only Indian woman to score a hat-trick at the Olympics
Awards: Arjuna Award 2021 | Padma Shri 2022 | Hockey India Dhanraj Pillay Award (Forward of Year 2021 & 2022)

Mahendra Gurjar — Para-Athletics World Record

Event: Men's F42 Javelin Throw
New world record: 61.17 metres (on 3rd attempt)
Previous record: 59.19 m (Brazil's Roberto Floriani Edenilson, 2022)
Tournament: 2025 Nottwil World Para Athletics Grand Prix, Switzerland (May 26, 2025)
Classification: F42 = moderate movement impairment in one leg

6. Asian Wrestling Championships 2025

Venue: Amman, Jordan
Manisha Bhanwala: Gold in women's 62 kg category (defeated Korea's Ok J Kim, 8–7) — India's first gold at this championship since 2021
Antim Panghal: Bronze in 53 kg (defeated Taipei's Meng H Hsieh by technical superiority)
India's overall haul: 1 Gold, 1 Silver, 6 Bronze (including 2 Greco-Roman)
