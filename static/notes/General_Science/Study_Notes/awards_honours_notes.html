Geoffrey Hinton (Physics 2024) = "Godfather of AI" — won for NEURAL NETWORKS, NOT for ChatGPT. Demis Hassabis (Chemistry 2024) is CEO of Google DeepMind — won for AlphaFold protein prediction. Han Kang is South Korean, NOT Chinese or Japanese — FIRST Asian woman to win Nobel Literature.
Key Connections — Nobel 2024:
  • Physics: Hinton nicknamed "Godfather of AI" — previously worked at Google, quit in 2023 over AI safety concerns
  • Chemistry: Demis Hassabis — CEO, Google DeepMind. AlphaFold predicted structures of all ~200 million known proteins
  • Peace: Nihon Hidankyo — Hibakusha (bomb-affected people) — survivors of Hiroshima (Aug 6, 1945) & Nagasaki (Aug 9, 1945)
  • Literature: Han Kang's works include "The Vegetarian" (2016 Man Booker International), "Human Acts"
  • Economics: Book "Why Nations Fail" by Acemoglu & Robinson is famous — argues inclusive institutions drive prosperity

Section 3: Bharat Ratna 2024 2024

5 Bharat Ratna awards announced (Feb 2024) — largest batch in one year: 2024లో ఒకే సంవత్సరంలో 5 భారత రత్న పురస్కారాలు ప్రకటించారు — ఒకే ఏడాదిలో అత్యధిక సంఖ్య.
RecipientKnown ForNote
Karpoori Thakur Bihar CM (twice); OBC rights champion; "Jan Nayak" Posthumous; announced on his birth anniversary (Jan 24)
L.K. Advani BJP co-founder; Deputy PM (1999-2004); Rath Yatra leader Oldest living Bharat Ratna recipient (96 years)
P.V. Narasimha Rao 9th PM of India (1991-96); 1991 Economic Liberalisation Posthumous; "Father of Indian Economic Reforms"
M.S. Swaminathan Father of India's Green Revolution; food security Posthumous; died Sept 28, 2023
Chaudhary Charan Singh 5th PM of India (1979-80); farmers' rights leader Posthumous; "Champion of Farmers"
PV Narasimha Rao was India's 9th PM (NOT 8th or 10th). MS Swaminathan = Green Revolution (NOT Blue Revolution — that's fisheries). Karpoori Thakur = "Jan Nayak" — NOT "Loknayak" (that's Jayaprakash Narayan). L.K. Advani is the ONLY living recipient from 2024 batch.
Bharat Ratna — Key Facts:
  • India's highest civilian honour — established 1954
  • Given for exceptional service to nation; can be awarded posthumously
  • Max 3 per year (rule) — 2024 was special exception with 5
  • Bharat Ratna does not carry any monetary grant
  • Displayed as a peepal leaf medallion

D. Gukesh — World Chess Champion 2024

  • Born: May 29, 2006; Chennai, Tamil Nadu
  • Became youngest World Chess Champion on December 12, 2024 in Singapore
  • Beat defending champion Ding Liren (China) in Game 14 of FIDE World Championship
  • Previous record: Garry Kasparov became champion at 22 years (1985)
  • Gukesh became Grandmaster at age 12 — youngest Indian GM at the time
