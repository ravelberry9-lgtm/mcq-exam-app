🇮🇳 India's G20 Presidency 2023 — Key Achievements

  • African Union (AU) admitted as permanent G20 member — India's biggest diplomatic win
  • New Delhi Declaration adopted by consensus — despite Russia-Ukraine divisions
  • Global Biofuels Alliance launched at India's initiative (USA, Brazil, India as founding members)
  • India's Digital Public Infrastructure (UPI, Aadhaar, CoWIN) model showcased globally
  • Theme "Vasudhaiva Kutumbakam" from Maha Upanishad — ancient Sanskrit text
  • India first developing nation to use G20 Presidency as foreign policy tool this effectively
🔑 G20 Structure:
  • 19 countries + EU + African Union = 21 members now (post-2023)
  • Accounts for ~85% of global GDP, 75% of trade, 65% of world population
  • G20 Presidency: rotates each year; host sets agenda
  • Troika: current + previous + next presidency work together
  • G20 was established in 1999; elevated to Leaders' Summit in 2008
  • No permanent secretariat — rotates with Presidency

🔥 G20 2026 — USA (Miami) 2026

  • South Africa handed G20 Presidency to USA on January 12, 2026
  • Trump administration hosts the 21st G20 Leaders' Summit
  • Tensions: Trump administration had disputes with South Africa over land reform policies during SA presidency (2025)
  • Focus likely: tariffs, AI regulation, energy transition under Trump
⚠️ Exam Traps: India hosted G20 in 2023 (NOT 2024). Theme = Vasudhaiva Kutumbakam (from Maha Upanishad, NOT Rigveda). AU joined G20 at India's Summit 2023. Brazil hosted 2024, South Africa 2025, USA 2026. G20 was established 1999 (NOT 2008 — it became Leaders' Summit in 2008).

16th BRICS Summit — Kazan 2024 | కజాన్ సదస్సు

Key outcomes of Kazan Summit (October 22-24, 2024):
  • BRICS+ format: Outreach sessions with partner nations; 13 countries given "Partner State" status
  • Modi-Putin bilateral: PM Modi met President Putin — India reaffirmed strategic autonomy despite western pressure
  • Modi-Xi bilateral: First formal bilateral between PM Modi and President Xi Jinping since Galwan Valley clash (June 2020) — 4 years later; LAC patrolling agreement reached
  • BRICS currency talk: No consensus on new BRICS currency; India & Brazil opposed replacing USD
  • Kazan Declaration adopted — focus on multipolar world order
  • NDB (New Development Bank) expansion discussed

🔥 BRICS 2026 — India Chair | భారత్ అధ్యక్షత

  • 18th BRICS Summit: September 12-13, 2026, New Delhi
  • India is chairing BRICS for the first time after 2024 expansion to 10 members

🇮🇳 India's Key Bilateral Meetings at BRICS Kazan 2024

  • Modi-Xi (Oct 23, 2024): First bilateral since Galwan 2020; agreed to resume LAC patrolling at friction points (Depsang, Demchok); "stabilisation of India-China relations is in interest of both countries and the world"
  • Modi-Putin (Oct 22, 2024): Discussed bilateral trade, Ukraine conflict; India urged peaceful resolution; energy cooperation; Modi: "This is not an era of war" (echoing speech at BRICS)
  • India's BRICS position: Support for multipolar world; opposed dollarisation alternatives; advocate for reformed global governance
⚠️ Exam Traps: 16th BRICS = Kazan, Russia (2024) — NOT Johannesburg. Modi-Xi first bilateral after Galwan = Kazan 2024. BRICS has 10 members now (not 5). 18th BRICS = India 2026, New Delhi, Sept 12-13. BRICS theme 2026 has BRICS acronym embedded.
