Ch84 — సంకీర్ణ ప్రభుత్వాలు | Coalition Government

Coalition Politics · NDA · UPA · Key Episodes | APPSC Group 2

0. Meaning — సంకీర్ణ ప్రభుత్వం అర్థం

Coalition Government నిర్వచనం

Coalition (సంకీర్ణ): రెండు లేదా అంతకంటే ఎక్కువ పార్టీలు కలిసి ప్రభుత్వం ఏర్పాటు చేయడం.

India: 1989 నుండి 2014 వరకు coalitions మాత్రమే — "Coalition Era" గా పిలుస్తారు.

CMP (Common Minimum Programme): coalition భాగస్వాముల మధ్య minimum consensus agenda.

1. Conditions for Coalition — పరిస్థితులు

Coalition కి కారణాలు

No single party majority in Lok Sabha (hung parliament).

Multi-party system, regional parties బలపడడం.

FPTP system: regional concentration + national fragmentation.

2. First Coalition (1977-89) — మొదటి సంకీర్ణ

Janata Party & After

Morarji Desai — మొదటి non-Congress Prime Minister (1977). Janata Party coalition.

Janata Party: Congress(O), BLD, Jan Sangh, Socialists merged — anti-Emergency coalition.

Charan Singh (1979): Congress(I) outside support తో PM — confidence vote కి ముందే resign.

Rajiv Gandhi 1984: 404 seats — Congress overwhelming majority.

3. Era of Instability (1989-1999)

అస్థిర ప్రభుత్వాలు

1989-1999: 5 Prime Ministers — V.P. Singh, Chandra Shekhar, Narasimha Rao, Deve Gowda, I.K. Gujral.

Vajpayee I (1998): NDA I fell by 1 vote (270-269) after AIADMK withdrawal.

United Front governments 1996-1998: Outside support from Congress/BJP.

4. NDA & UPA Era (1999-2014)

Stable Coalitions

NDA II (1999-2004): Vajpayee — first full-term coalition government (5 years complete).

UPA I (2004-2009): Manmohan Singh — Congress + regional parties + Left (outside support).

UPA II (2009-2014): Manmohan Singh — Congress strengthened; Left withdrew support 2008 (nuclear deal) but government survived.

5. Post-2014 — BJP Era

Majority vs Coalition

BJP 2014: 282 seats (majority alone) — "end of coalition compulsion" for BJP.

BJP 2019: 303 seats — stronger majority.

BJP 2024: 240 seats (below 272 majority) → dependent on TDP (16) + JDU (12) for NDA III.

INDIA (Indian National Developmental Inclusive Alliance) formed 2023 — opposition coalition.

6. Merits & Demerits — లాభాలు & నష్టాలు

Coalition Pros & Cons

లాభాలు: Broader representation, regional interests addressed, checks on majority tyranny.

నష్టాలు: Instability, policy paralysis, blackmail by small partners, CMP lowest common denominator.

7. Key Facts — త్వరిత పునశ్చరణ
First non-Congress PM = Morarji Desai 1977 NDA I fell = 1 vote (270-269) Vajpayee NDA II = first full-term coalition BJP 2014 = 282 seats (majority alone) BJP 2024 = 240 seats (NDA dependent) INDIA Alliance = 2023 CMP = Common Minimum Programme
