🗺️ 4. 6వ Schedule — Tribal Areas (NE States)
6వ Schedule — NE రాష్ట్రాలలో Autonomous Districts
Applicable: Assam, Meghalaya, Tripura, Mizoram (4 రాష్ట్రాలు)
Autonomous District Councils (ADCs): Legislation అధికారం కలిగి ఉంటాయి
ADC: land management, social customs, money-lending, forests (limited)
Governor Autonomous Districts divide, merge, create చేయవచ్చు
ADC Court: Civil + Criminal cases (Indian Penal Code/CrPC apply)
Assam: Bodo Territorial Council, Karbi Anglong, Dima Hasao
Meghalaya: Garo Hills, Khasi Hills, Jaintia Hills
Mizoram: Chakma, Mara, Lai Autonomous District Councils
