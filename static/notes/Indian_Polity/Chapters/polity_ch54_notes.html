Chapter 54 — National Commission for Scheduled Castes (NCSC)

జాతీయ షెడ్యూల్డ్ కులాల కమిషన్ | Article 338 of the Constitution

0. Historical Background & Evolution

నేపథ్యం

Constitutional safeguards SC కోసం: Articles 15, 16, 17 (untouchability abolition), 46.

Article 338: Original 1950 — SC Commissioner నియమించాలి.

65th Amendment 1990: Article 338 అమెండ్ చేసి NCSC+ST (combined) ఏర్పాటు.

89th Amendment 2003: SC మరియు ST కోసం separate commissions — NCSC (Art.338) మరియు NCST (Art.338A).

1. Article 338 & Constitutional Framework

Article 338

Article 338(1): National Commission for Scheduled Castes — Constitutional body.

89th Amendment 2003 వరకు: SC+ST combined commission.

Article 338(5): NCSC powers — SC safeguards investigate, monitor, evaluate, advise.

Article 338(9): Annual Report రాష్ట్రపతికి → Parliament.

Article 338(10): NCSC powers as determined by Parliament.

2. NCSC Composition & Appointment

సంఘటన

Chairperson: 1

Vice-Chairperson: 1

Members: 3

మొత్తం: 5 (Article 338(1))

President నియమిస్తారు.

3. Tenure, Removal & Service Conditions

పదవీకాలం

Chairperson, VP, Members: 3 సంవత్సరాలు.

Re-appointment eligible.

తొలగింపు: President — SC inquiry తర్వాత (misbehaviour/incapacity).

Salaries: Consolidated Fund of India (charged).

4. Powers & Functions of NCSC

ముఖ్య విధులు

SC safeguards (Arts. 15, 16, 17, 46, reservations) నెరవేరుతున్నాయా investigate, monitor, evaluate.

SC complaints — suo motu లేదా complaint మేరకు.

Civil Court powers.

Socio-economic development recommendations.

Annual Report President కి.

5. NCSC and Atrocities / Protection Laws

Atrocities

SC & ST (Prevention of Atrocities) Act 1989 — NCSC implementation monitor చేస్తుంది.

Untouchability violations (Protection of Civil Rights Act 1955) — NCSC investigate చేయగలదు.

Article 17: Untouchability abolished — enforcement NCSC monitor చేస్తుంది.

6. SC Reservation Framework

Reservation

SC reservation: 15% Central services + Central educational institutions.

Carry forward rule: unfilled SC vacancies carry forward to next year.

SC promotions reservation: Article 16(4A).

Creamy Layer: SC కి apply కాదు — OBC కి మాత్రమే.

SC list: President specifies — Article 341.
