💧 1. అంతర్-రాష్ట్ర జల వివాదాలు — Art.262
Art.262 నిబంధనలు
Art.262(1): Parliament జల వివాదాలపై చట్టాలు చేయవచ్చు
Art.262(2): Parliament, SC & HC jurisdiction exclude చేయవచ్చు

🏛️ 2. అంతర్-రాష్ట్ర మండలి — Art.263
Art.263 — Inter-State Council
Art.263: రాష్ట్రపతి ISC ఏర్పాటు చేయవచ్చు (Advisory)
ISC స్థాపన: జూన్ 1990 (Sarkaria Commission సిఫారసుపై)
Chairperson: Prime Minister
సభ్యులు: అన్ని రాష్ట్రాల CM లు + కేంద్ర పాలిత ప్రాంత LGs/Admins
6 Cabinet Ministers (Centre): PM నామినేట్ చేస్తారు
ISC రంగాలు: Coordination + Disputes + Common interest items
ISC కేవలం Recommendatory body మాత్రమే
ISC Standing Committee Chairperson: Home Minister
🗺️ 3. జోనల్ కౌన్సిల్స్
5 Zonal Councils — States Reorganisation Act, 1956
Northern Zone: J&K, HP, Punjab, Haryana, Rajasthan, Delhi, Chandigarh (7 members)
Central Zone: UP, Uttarakhand, MP, Chhattisgarh (4)
Eastern Zone: Bihar, Jharkhand, Odisha, WB (4)
Western Zone: Gujarat, Goa, Maharashtra, Dadra & NH, D&D (5)
Southern Zone: AP, Telangana, Karnataka, Kerala, TN, Puducherry (6)
North-Eastern Council: 8 States (అలాదా — North-Eastern Council Act 1971)
Zonal Council నిర్మాణం
Chairperson: Union Home Minister (అన్ని 5 Zonal Councils కి)
Vice-Chairperson: Zone రాష్ట్రాల CM లు (rotation by year)
సభ్యులు: CM + 2 Cabinet Ministers (ప్రతి రాష్ట్రం నుండి)
Advisory (Consultative) body — binding కాదు
📜 4. Art.261 — Full Faith & Credit
Articleవిషయం
Art.261(1)Public acts, records, judicial proceedings of Centre/States — full faith & credit throughout India
Art.261(2)Parliament can prescribe manner of proof and effect
Art.261(3)Final judgments/orders of civil courts — executable throughout India
Art.262Inter-State Water Disputes — Parliament can legislate + exclude SC/HC
Art.263Inter-State Council — రాష్ట్రపతి అనుమతితో; coordination + disputes
🛒 5. వాణిజ్య స్వేచ్ఛ — Art.301–307
Art.301–307 (Part XIII)
Art.301: Trade, Commerce & Intercourse — free throughout India
Art.302: Parliament can impose restrictions in public interest
Art.303: Parliament/States — no preference/discrimination among States
Art.304(a): States can tax goods from other States equally
Art.304(b): States can impose reasonable restrictions (President's prior sanction needed)
Art.307: Parliament can appoint authority for Art.301–304
ముఖ్య Restrictions
Art.301 = Freedom of Trade — Part XIII ముఖ్యం
Art.304(b) Restrictions: State Legislature → President's prior sanction తప్పనిసరి
Art.302: Public Interest — Parliament కి restriction authority
Art.303 Exception: Scarcity of goods cases లో preference allowed
🤝 6. రాష్ట్రాల మధ్య సమన్వయం
Mechanisms for Inter-State Coordination
Art.261: Full Faith & Credit — civil court decrees execute everywhere
Art.262: Water Disputes Tribunal — court jurisdiction excluded
Art.263: ISC — PM Chairman; advisory body
Zonal Councils: Regional coordination (Home Minister chairs all)
National Integration Council: PM chairs; minority, communal issues
GST Council (Art.279A): Fiscal coordination Centre + States
NITI Aayog Governing Council: PM + All CMs
NDC (dissolved): Plan coordination (pre-2014)
