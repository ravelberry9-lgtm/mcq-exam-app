Chapter 32 — పబ్లిక్ సర్వీసెస్ / Public Services & UPSC

Art.315–323 | UPSC | SPSC | Joint PSC | Part XIV

Section 1 — Art.315–316: PSC Constitution & Appointment
Art.315(1): Union కి = Union Public Service Commission (UPSC); ప్రతి State కి = State Public Service Commission (SPSC). రెండింటినీ రాజ్యాంగం mandate చేస్తుంది.
Art.315(2): రెండు లేదా అంతకంటే ఎక్కువ States → Parliament law ద్వారా Joint State PSC (JSPSC) ఏర్పాటు చేయవచ్చు.
Art.316(1): UPSC Chairman + Members = President నియమిస్తారు. SPSC Chairman + Members = Governor నియమిస్తారు.
Half-rule: UPSC/SPSC లో కనీసం సగం members = Union/State Government service లో 10 సంవత్సరాలు పనిచేసిన వారు.
UPSC Chairman tenure: 6 సంవత్సరాలు లేదా 65 సంవత్సరాల వయస్సు వరకు (whichever earlier). SPSC Chairman = 6 సంవత్సరాలు లేదా 62 సంవత్సరాల వయస్సు వరకు.
Section 2 — Art.317–318: Removal & Conditions
Art.317(1): UPSC/SPSC Chairman/Member తొలగింపు = President ఆదేశంపై — కానీ Supreme Court inquiry తర్వాత మాత్రమే (SC recommendation).
Art.317(3): Immediate removal (without SC inquiry) కారణాలు: (a) దివాలా తీయడం; (b) office of profit hold చేయడం; (c) infirmity of mind or body; (d) misconduct (President can suspend pending SC inquiry).
Art.318: Regulations — UPSC: President; SPSC: Governor. Members సంఖ్య, service conditions నిర్ణయిస్తారు.
SPSC Chairman: Governor తొలగించలేరు — only President తొలగిస్తారు (SC inquiry తర్వాత). SPSC independence safeguard.
Section 3 — Art.319–320: Functions & Prohibitions
Art.319: UPSC Chairman → retire తర్వాత Union/State Government లో ఏ employment కి అర్హులు కారు (పూర్తి ban). UPSC Member → UPSC Chairman లేదా SPSC Chairman అవుతారు.
Art.319: SPSC Chairman → UPSC Member లేదా వేరే SPSC Chairman అవుతారు. SPSC Member → UPSC Member, UPSC Chairman, వేరే SPSC Chairman అవుతారు.
Art.320(1): UPSC/SPSC విధులు: Civil service examinations conduct + Appointments + Promotions + Transfers on merit + Disciplinary matters advise.
Art.320(3): Consultation with UPSC mandatory: civil service appointments, promotions, transfers, disciplinary matters. కానీ Government UPSC advice follow చేయాల్సిన obligation లేదు.
Section 4 — Art.321–323: Extension, Reports & Exemptions
Art.321: Parliament/State Legislature law ద్వారా UPSC/SPSC కి అదనపు functions extend చేయవచ్చు (e.g. local bodies, corporations కి recruitment).
Art.322: UPSC expenses = Consolidated Fund of India కి charge. SPSC = State Consolidated Fund కి charge.
Art.323: Annual Report — UPSC → President కి submit; President Parliament లో lay చేయిస్తారు + explanatory memorandum. SPSC → Governor కి; Governor State Legislature లో.
Art.323A/323B: Administrative Tribunals (42nd Amendment 1976) — PSC jurisdiction ని override చేయగలవు కొన్ని matters లో.
Section 5 — UPSC నిర్మాణం & Features
Established: October 1, 1926 — Federal Public Service Commission గా British India లో. Constitution of India లో UPSC గా రాజ్యాంగ హోదా పొందింది.
Chairman + Members: President నియమిస్తారు. ప్రస్తుతం: 1 Chairman + 10 members (usually). Minimum = 50% government servants with 10 years experience.
Tenure: 6 years or 65 years of age (whichever earlier). Re-appointment లేదు.
Removal: Only by President on SC inquiry. Direct removal by President only in 4 specified cases (Art.317(3)).
Independence safeguards: Removal only via SC inquiry; Expenses on CFI; No re-appointment; Post-retirement no government employment (Chairman).
Section 6 — UPSC విధులు & పరీక్షలు
Civil Services Examination (CSE): IAS, IPS, IFS మొదలైన 24 services కి recruitment. Prelims + Mains + Interview.
Other exams: NDA, CDS, CAPF, IES/ISS, IFS (Indian Forest Service — separate exam), CISF AC (LDCE) etc. Note: SCRA was discontinued after 2014.
Art.320(3) mandatory consultation: Appointments to civil services/posts; Promotions; Transfers; Disciplinary matters (dismissal/removal/reduction); Claims for costs in legal proceedings; Pension cases.
UPSC cannot be consulted for: Reservations for SC/ST (President determines); Certain temporary/part-time posts; President specifies (Art.320(3) exceptions).
Advisory role: UPSC advice = advisory (not binding). Government can deviate with reasons recorded.
Section 7 — State PSC & Joint PSC
SPSC: Art.315 — every State must have SPSC. Governor appoints Chairman + Members. Tenure = 6 years or 62 years (whichever earlier).
SPSC removal: Only President (not Governor) — SC inquiry mandatory. Governor can suspend pending inquiry.
Joint State PSC (JSPSC): Art.315(2) — Parliament law ద్వారా 2+ States కోసం. Chairman = President appoints. Expenses = CFI కి charge.
JSPSC history: Only one example — Punjab + Haryana JSPSC (1966, States Reorganisation). Haryana separate SPSC ఏర్పాటు చేసుకున్నాక terminate అయింది.
National Recruitment Agency (NRA): 2020 — Common Eligibility Test (CET) — SSC, RRB, IBPS మొదలైన screening test. UPSC వేరు — NRA UPSC కాదు.
Section 8 — Comparisons & Key Facts
UPSC vs SPSC: UPSC Chairman removed by President (SC inquiry); SPSC Chairman also removed by President only (not Governor). Both = independent constitutional bodies.
Post-retirement restrictions: UPSC Chairman = cannot hold any government employment. UPSC Member = can become Chairman of UPSC or any SPSC. SPSC Chairman = UPSC Member or another SPSC Chair.
UPSC Secretariat: New Delhi. UPSC = constitutional body (Part XIV, Art.315–323).
Note — Art.323A Tribunals: Art.320 matters partially superseded by Administrative Tribunals Act 1985 for certain service matters (CAT).
