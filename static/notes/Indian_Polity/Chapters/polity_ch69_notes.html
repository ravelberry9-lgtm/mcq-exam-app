0. PIL అర్థం | Meaning of PIL

PIL నిర్వచనం

PIL (Public Interest Litigation): వ్యక్తిగత ప్రయోజనం కాదు — ప్రజా ప్రయోజనం కోసం court లో petition.

సాధారణ litigation: only aggrieved party file చేయగలరు (locus standi).

PIL: ఏ public-spirited citizen అయినా file చేయవచ్చు — relaxed locus standi.

SC: Art.32; HC: Art.226 కింద file చేయవచ్చు.

1. PIL చరిత్ర | History

Evolution of PIL in India

PIL development: 1970s-1980s — Justice P.N. Bhagwati & V.R. Krishna Iyer నేతృత్వంలో.

Hussainara Khatoon v. Bihar 1979: మొదటి PIL — Bihar undertrial prisoners.

S.P. Gupta v. UOI 1981: Locus standi broadened — "any member of public".

Epistolary Jurisdiction: postcard/letter = writ petition — Justice Bhagwati pioneered.

2. PIL రకాలు | Types

PIL Categories

Environmental PILs: MC Mehta cases — Ganga pollution, Taj Mahal, Delhi CNG.

Social Justice PILs: Bandhua Mukti Morcha 1984 (bonded labour), Olga Tellis 1985 (pavement dwellers).

Governance PILs: Vineet Narain 1997 (CBI independence), PUCL v. UOI (right to food).

Electoral PILs: ADR v. UOI 2002 (candidate disclosure), PUCL 2003 (NOTA).

3. PIL Process — ప్రక్రియ

How PIL Works

Nominal/no court fee — పేదల కోసం accessible.

Court Commissioners appoint చేయవచ్చు — ground-level fact-finding కోసం.

Continuing Mandamus: court compliance monitor చేస్తూ case retain చేస్తుంది.

Amicus Curiae: court independent expert appoint చేయవచ్చు.

4. PIL సమస్యలు | PIL Problems

Criticism of PIL

PIL industry: vested interests private gain కోసం PIL misuse.

Counter-majoritarian problem: unelected judges policy decisions చేయడం.

Frivolous PILs: courts costs impose చేస్తున్నాయి.

Judicial overreach vs genuine public interest balance అవసరం.
