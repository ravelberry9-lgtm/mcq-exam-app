Ch88 — విదేశాంగ విధానం | Foreign Policy of India

NAM · Panchsheel · India-USA · SAARC · Post-Cold War | APPSC Group 2

0. Meaning & Determinants — అర్థం & నిర్ణాయక అంశాలు

Foreign Policy అర్థం

Foreign Policy: భారతదేశం తన జాతీయ ప్రయోజనాలు సాధించడానికి ఇతర దేశాలతో వ్యవహరించే విధానం.

రాజ్యాంగ ఆధారం: Art.51 (DPSP) — international peace, security, respect for international law.

Determinants: geography, history, economy, military capability, leadership, ideology.

1. Nehru Era — నెహ్రూ కాలం (1947-1964)

Nehruvian Foreign Policy

Panchsheel (పంచశీల — 5 Principles): 1954 India-China Tibet Agreement.

5 Principles: Mutual respect (పరస్పర గౌరవం), Non-aggression, Non-interference, Equality, Peaceful coexistence.

NAM (Non-Aligned Movement): Belgrade 1961 — Nehru, Nasser, Tito, Sukarno, Nkrumah.

Bandung Conference 1955 — Asia-Africa solidarity.

India-China War 1962 — Panchsheel failure highlighted.

2. Non-Alignment & NAM

NAM Principles

Cold War లో USA లేదా USSR — ఏ bloc తో చేరకపోవడం.

NAM 1st Summit: Belgrade 1961 (25 countries).

India: NAM founding member; Nehru, Indira Gandhi, Rajiv Gandhi NAM chairs.

Post-Cold War: NAM relevance questioned — India "strategic autonomy" adopt చేసింది.

3. Neighbours & South Asia

Regional Relations

SAARC: South Asian Association for Regional Cooperation — 1985; HQ Kathmandu; 8 members.

Shimla Agreement 1972 (India-Pakistan) — bilateral issues bilaterally settle చేయాలి.

Galwan Valley clash 2020 (India-China) — 20 Indian soldiers killed.

India-Bangladesh: 100th Amendment 2015 — Land Boundary Agreement (LBA) అమలు.

BIMSTEC: Bay of Bengal Initiative — India + Bangladesh + Myanmar + Sri Lanka + Thailand + Nepal + Bhutan.

4. Major Powers — ప్రధాన శక్తులతో సంబంధాలు

Key Bilateral Relations

Indo-Soviet Treaty 1971 (20-year Peace, Friendship & Cooperation) — Bangladesh liberation war context.

Pokhran I (1974): Smiling Buddha — peaceful nuclear explosion.

Pokhran II (1998): Operation Shakti — nuclear tests; NFU (No First Use) declared.

Indo-US Nuclear Deal 2008 (123 Agreement) — India-specific safeguards with IAEA.

5. Multilateral Forums

India in Multilateral Forums

SCO (Shanghai Cooperation Organisation): India full member since 2017.

G20: India hosted 2023 — "Vasudhaiva Kutumbakam" theme.

QUAD: India-USA-Japan-Australia — Indo-Pacific strategy.

ISA (International Solar Alliance): India-France joint initiative 2015 — COP21 Paris.

CDRI (Coalition for Disaster Resilient Infrastructure): India led — 2019.

BRICS: Brazil, Russia, India, China, South Africa — India founding member.

6. Post-Cold War & Modi Era

Modern Foreign Policy

Look East → Act East Policy: Narasimha Rao era నుండి; Modi era లో "Act East" గా upgrade.

Neighbourhood First Policy: India's priority to South Asian neighbours.

G20 India Presidency 2023: 83 outcomes; Africa Union added as permanent member.

India-Middle East-Europe Economic Corridor (IMEC): G20 2023 లో announced.
