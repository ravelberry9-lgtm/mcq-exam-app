Chapter 34 — కంప్ట్రోలర్ & ఆడిటర్ జనరల్ / Comptroller & Auditor General

Art.148–151 | Appointment | Audit Types | PAC | Independence | Part V

Section 1 — Art.148: Appointment & Removal
Art.148(1): CAG = President నియమిస్తారు — warrant under hand and seal. Constitutional office.
Removal: CAG = SC judge మాదిరిగా — President + Parliament address (both Houses, special majority: 2/3 members present+voting) on grounds of proved misbehaviour or incapacity.
Tenure: 6 సంవత్సరాలు లేదా 65 సంవత్సరాల వయస్సు — whichever earlier. Re-appointment లేదు.
Art.148(4): Service conditions = Parliament by law. 1971 Act (CAG Duties, Powers & Conditions of Service) ద్వారా.
Art.148(5): President/Governor instructions పై audit staff = CAG కింద పని చేస్తారు.
Section 2 — Art.149–151: CAG Functions
Art.149: CAG = Union + States + other authorities audit. Parliament law ద్వారా duties/powers నిర్ణయం.
Art.150: Union + States accounts = President advice on CAG recommendation ఆధారంగా kept. Accounts format = CAG prescribes.
Art.151(1): Union accounts audit reports = President కి submit → Parliament కి lay చేయాలి (PAC పరిశీలన).
Art.151(2): State accounts audit reports = Governor కి submit → State Legislature కి lay చేయాలి.
CAG Act 1971: CAG duties/powers specific గా define — audit scope, certification, performance audit.
Section 3 — Types of Audit
Regularity Audit (Compliance Audit): Government spending = rules, laws, sanctions కి conform అవుతున్నదా? Traditional/classical audit.
Propriety Audit: Expenditure = prudent, wise, wasteful కాదా? Financial propriety check. Discretionary grants scrutiny.
Performance Audit (Value-for-Money): Government programs/schemes = 3Es: Economy, Efficiency, Effectiveness. Modern audit.
Certification Audit: Accounts correct + complete గా certify — Finance Accounts + Appropriation Accounts.
Commercial Audit: Government companies/corporations (PSEs) — even if Chartered Accountants audit, CAG supplementary audit.
Section 4 — CAG & Parliament
Public Accounts Committee (PAC): CAG audit reports examine చేసే Parliament committee. 22 members (15 LS + 7 RS). Opposition leader traditionally chairs. Finance Minister = NOT member.
Estimates Committee: Expenditure estimates scrutinize — future spending. CAG = past spending audit (PAC). రెండూ different.
Committee on Public Undertakings (COPU): PSUs audit reports examine — CAG commercial audit reports.
CAG = Parliament's watchdog: Executive (government) spending = Parliament's behalf audit చేస్తుంది. Accountability mechanism.
Appropriation Accounts: Budget vs. Actual expenditure comparison — CAG certifies annually.
Section 5 — Independence Safeguards
Security of Tenure: Removal = SC judge procedure (difficult, Parliament address + special majority).
Salary from CFI: CAG salary = SC judge equivalent, charged to Consolidated Fund of India — Parliament vote కి subject కాదు.
Service conditions not alterable: Art.148(4) — conditions cannot be varied disadvantageously after appointment.
No re-appointment: Prevents bias — CAG future favor కోసం government కి please చేయలేరు.
Post-retirement bar: Art.148(4): CAG = Union/State Government కింద further office ineligible. Independence complete.
Section 6 — CAG Jurisdiction
CAG audits: (1) Consolidated Fund of India; (2) Consolidated Fund of States; (3) Consolidated Fund of UTs with Legislature; (4) Contingency Fund; (5) Public Account; (6) Government companies (supplementary audit); (7) Govt-aided bodies (50%+ aid).
CAG does NOT audit: (1) Panchayats (State Audit); (2) Municipalities (State Audit); (3) Private companies; (4) Bodies not receiving government funds; (5) RBI (statutory corporation with own auditors).
Autonomous bodies: Substantially funded by government = CAG audit. Universities = mostly State Auditor General.
Defence Audit: Defence Accounts = CAG audit (through Defence Accounts Department).
Section 7 — CAG vs UPSC vs ECI
Appointment: All three = President appoints. CAG = warrant under hand+seal (Art.148(1)).
Removal: CAG = SC judge like (Parliament address + special majority). UPSC Chairman = SC inquiry. CEC = SC judge like. ECs = President + CEC recommendation.
Tenure: CAG = 6 yrs/65 yrs. UPSC = 6 yrs/65 yrs. CEC/EC = 6 yrs/65 yrs. All same!
Salary: All = SC judge equivalent, charged to CFI.
Re-appointment: CAG = NO. UPSC Chairman = NO. CEC = NO. EC = NO.
Post-retirement bar: CAG = YES (government office). UPSC Chairman = YES. CEC = YES. ECs = no explicit bar.
Section 8 — Important Facts & Cases
CAG described as: Dr. B.R. Ambedkar — "most important officer in the Constitution." Guardian of public purse.
First CAG of India: V. Narahari Rao (1948–1954).
CAG + Accountant General: Earlier CAG both compiled accounts AND audited. Since 1976 (separation of accounts from audit) — accounting to Ministry of Finance (Controller General of Accounts); CAG = audit only.
Comptroller vs. Auditor: In India, CAG = no real "Comptroller" function (doesn't sanction/control expenditure before it's incurred — unlike UK Comptroller). India CAG = primarily post-facto auditor.
INTOSAI: International Organisation of Supreme Audit Institutions — CAG of India is member.
