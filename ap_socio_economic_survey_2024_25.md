## Chapter 1: General Overview

**Employment (PLFS 2023-24):**

- Rural LFPR: 50.1%, Urban LFPR: 41.3%
- Employment by sector: Agriculture 45.6%, Manufacturing 22.8%, Construction 12.3%, Services 31.6%

**Unemployment:**
- Overall: 4.1%
- Rural: 3.4%, Urban: 5.9%
- Youth (15-29): 17.5%
- Female: 6.9%, Male: 4.3%
- Informal sector: 62.3% of rural, 45.6% of urban employment
